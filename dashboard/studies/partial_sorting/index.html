Question & approach

Does violating the Young condition (large gamma_ld=7.5) stall sorting at a partially-sorted, trapped state?
Light cells cannot fully surround dark; clusters trap heterotypic inclusions and never form a monolayer, sorting logarithmically slow.
Swap J_ll/J_ld so gamma_ld=7.5, simulate from a random IC to 2000 MCS, and compare against normal sorting to demonstrate incomplete sorting.
